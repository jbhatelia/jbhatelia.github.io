About

Welcome!

     I am currently Assistant Manager (G.E.T. - BOND Program) at Jio Platforms Limited & a Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Information Technology from Dwarkadas J. Sanghvi College of Engineering (Autonomous under University of Mumbai) .

I’m also one of The Youngest EC-Council Certified Security Analyst (ECSA v10) holders in the World.

I’m also a Certified Ethical Hacker (C|EH v9) by EC-Council and am also certified by the Maharashtra Self-Employment Training Board in the field of "Ethical Hacking & Cellular Security".

I also have knowledge about ISO/IEC 27001:2013 (Information Security Management). & am a Expert Witness for Mumbai Police (Cyber Crime Cell).

I worked as an Information Security Intern at Skynet Secure Solutions from June 2018 to July 2018.

I have also successfully conducted a 2 day “Internet Security Workshop” on “Ethical Hacking & Cellular Security” which was organised by ICWiCOM 2019 at D.J. Sanghvi College of Engineering.

I have also conducted workshop on “Information Security” at Computer Society of India (CSI), Mumbai Office along with the Skynet Team.

I worked on “Post-Disaster Management System using Blockchain” with C-DAC (Centre for Development of Advanced Computing).

Recently, I was selected for the ACM India Summer School 2019 on Detection & Analysis of Malware by ACM India & Quick Heal Technologies.



I aim to become a CISO.
